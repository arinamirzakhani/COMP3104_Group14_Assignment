George Brown College is a public college of applied arts and technology located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. 
The college offers a wide range of diploma, degree, certificate, and continuing education programs. 
It has three main campuses: St. James, Casa Loma, and Waterfront. 
George Brown is known for its strong industry partnerships, hands-on learning opportunities, and emphasis on preparing students for the workforce. 
The college also supports applied research projects and collaboration with local businesses and organizations.
